## 1. About the App

GoldenScope is a photography planning tool that provides estimated terrain analysis, sun and moon positioning data, weather forecasts, and location suggestions to assist photographers in finding potential photo opportunities. The App uses publicly available data sources including elevation data, OpenStreetMap geographic features, and weather forecasts to generate its suggestions.

The App relies on third-party data sources that may be incomplete, outdated, or inaccurate. Specifically:
